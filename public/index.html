Marinho Barcellos

Senior Lecturer

University of Waikato

Marinho Barcellos is a professor at the School of Computer and Mathematical Sciences at University of Waikato and a member of the WAND network research group. His research interests include Internet measurements, programmable data planes and network security.

He was an Adjunct and later Associate Professor at INF/UFRGS between 2010 and 2019 and recently moved to the University of Waikato.

Interests

  • Internet measurements
  • Programmable data planes
  • Network security
